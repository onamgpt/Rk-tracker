Our Story

A Legacy Lit by
Devotion

What began in 1922 as National Flag Perfumery Works - a small family enterprise in Bangalore - grew into one of India's most celebrated incense houses over the next hundred years.

In 1972, a new chapter opened: Onam Agarbathi Pvt. Ltd. was born, and with it came the Vaishak brand - synonymous with quality, innovation, and the daily ritual of prayer across the subcontinent.

Three national packaging awards for transformable box innovations cemented Onam's reputation not just as fragrance makers, but as craftspeople who honour the sacred with every design.
